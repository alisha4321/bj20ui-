About the Event

What is Cit-E Cycling?

Cit-E Cycling is a national cycling competition where individuals and cycling groups from across UK cities compete against each other on high-tech exercise bikes. Winners from each city will then compete in a national event to be crowned the overall UK champion.

🚴

Individual Entry

Enter the competition on your own and compete for prizes in your age category and gender. Each participant is only allowed to enter once.

👥

Group Entry

Join a cycling club and compete as a team. Club scores are combined to find the best performing cycling group in the competition.

📊

Performance Tracking

Our 20 high-tech exercise bikes track your power output in watts and distance travelled in kilometres to rank all participants.
